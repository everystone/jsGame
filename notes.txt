blue lines: walls
green lines: doors ( enemies cant open, player moves through)



gameplay:

player starts with X cm wall, can build whatever, base. can place doors ( green line ). 
day/night cycle. day = white background, night = black background.
zombies spawn at night. Uses pathfinding to try to get to the player. Players must come out during day to harvest more resources ( walls, doors ).

Crafting: traps,



pvp: 

players must try to maze zombies into the others base.
